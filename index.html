Achasta Golf Dahlonega

Come Play Golf At Achasta

Achasta's Jack Nicklaus Signature golf course in Dahlonega is located in the foothills of the North Georgia Mountains. The Chestatee River winds and curls into play for more than half the holes. Golf Dahlonegas best luxury course and community.

Achasta has been selected to host:

  • PGA Tour's AT&T Classic Qualifier
  • Bell South Classic Open Qualifier
  • Georgia State Amateur Qualifier
  • US Open Qualifier
  • Georgia State Mid-Am Qualifier.
