Celebrate with Us

Achasta is a true hidden gem nestled in the foothills of the north Georgia Mountains. A community offering the best of both worlds. World class golf & luxurious amenities while only being minutes from the hallmark town of dahlonega. But also the graceful sense of peace and tranquility being surrounded by breathtaking views and the natural ambiance that overcomes anyone looking to get away from the stresses of everyday life. For those with a thirst for the great outdoors & adventure, Achasta is the best place to feel connected with nature. Featuring activities such as kayaking, nature trails, bike paths, fishing & more. It’s hard to believe that this majestic paradise is only 1 hour away from the city of atlanta. Making it the perfect place for a second home or full time residence. Everyone that comes to achasta falls in love and we know you will to! Come experience everything Achasta has to offer today.

Come Play Golf At Achasta

Achasta’s Jack Nicklaus Signature golf course in Dahlonega is located in the foothills of the North Georgia Mountains. The Chestatee River winds and curls into play for more than half the holes. Golf Dahlonegas best luxury course and community.

  • Georgia State Amateur Qualifier
  • US Open Qualifier
  • Georgia State Mid-Am Qualifier.
