Golf Etiquette & Dress

This is a helpful guideline on the manner in which the game of golf should be played. If they are followed, all players will gain maximum enjoyment from the game. The overriding principle is that consideration should be shown to others on the course at all times.

Etiquette

Report to the tee at your designated tee time
Please observe Golf Cart Rules (Cart Path Only or 90 Degree Rule)
You must have a drivers license to drive a cart Please observe “no cart signs” and keep the cart out of those areas.

Care of the Course

Please repair your ball marks and fill your divots with sand. (Note: please do not replace the divot).
Please rake the bunker when exiting and leave the rake with the handle out and the head in the bunker. Observe the pace of play rules. You are required to play 18 holes in 4 hours 15 minutes.

Dress Code

Dress code policies apply to all adults, children and guests. Members are responsible for the attire of their children and guests. Members should inform their guests of the dress code prior to their arrival at the club.

  • No Denim allowed
  • A collared golf shirt is required (All shirts MUST BE tucked in)
  • No steel spikes allowed soft spikes only
  • Hats must be worn straight in a traditional fashion
