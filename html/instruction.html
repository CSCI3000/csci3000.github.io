Golf Instruction

The Achasta Golf Club offers a full array of instruction for all ages and levels of golfers. Achasta has 1 PGA Golf Professional on staff to help enhance your golfing experience. Instructional programs include Junior Golf Camps and Clinics, Ladies Beginner Clinics and one on one programs for all ages. The lesson stations are equipped with video, teaching aids and tools. The Achasta Golf Professional staff is passionate about helping golfers understand good golf etiquette and technique. Call the golf shop at 706-867-7900 or email melliott@achasta.com for lesson availability.
